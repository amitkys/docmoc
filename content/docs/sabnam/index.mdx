## Introduction

AI Integrated set of Test series for students preparing for board exams and other competitive exams.

It have test-series:

- By [previous years](https://sabnam.amitkys.in/past10year?topic=NCERT&clas=10th&subject=Math)
- By [chapters](https://sabnam.amitkys.in/exams?type=cs&subject=os&chapter=series)
- By [topics](https://sabnam.amitkys.in/subjects)
